Interpreting Job Status:

  • not_found: Job has not been submitted to the proML cluster.
  • data_upload: Job data is being uploaded from the user.
  • queued: Job has been submitted for processing.
  • model_train: Job is currently training model.
  • job_execute: Job is executing against trained model.
  • closing: Job data is being written to storage
  • complete: Job results are available.
  • failed: Job did not complete successfully.
